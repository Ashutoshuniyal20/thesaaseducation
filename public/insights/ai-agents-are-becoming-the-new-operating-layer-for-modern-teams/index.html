AI Agents Are Becoming the New Operating Layer for Modern Teams

A practical look at where agentic software creates leverage and where it still needs a careful human hand.

MMaya ChenMay 28, 2026 · 1 min read

AI agents are moving from experiments into everyday operations. The useful question is no longer whether teams will adopt them, but which processes are ready first.

Start with bounded work

The best early use cases have clear inputs, observable outcomes, and a straightforward review step. That makes research, reporting, and routine coordination strong places to begin.

Keep judgment visible

Teams get more value when they treat agents as an operational layer with clear ownership rather than a replacement for expertise.
