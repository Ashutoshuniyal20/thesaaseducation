SEO software should turn search opportunities into clear priorities. The strongest stacks cover research, technical health, content performance, and reporting without creating another layer of operational noise.

## Start with the decisions

Choose tools around the questions your team needs to answer each week. A smaller stack with clear ownership is more useful than a broad collection of overlapping dashboards.
